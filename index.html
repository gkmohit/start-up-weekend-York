AMUZA - Your hobbie finder

Take your hobbies to the
next level!

We have finally built an app for you to plug new activities to your life. Learn things according to what you need and earn rewards. It is now time to gamify the process of learning new things.

App's main features

Meeting new people and connecting to learn new hobies is the best way to catch a new skill. We have streamlined great activities for you and around you. You don't have to spend hours looking for what to do anymore.

Focused Around You

We have removed the process of you having to search for things to do around you, but we only show you what is happening around you.

Smart Suggestion

Using your past activites and our robust algorithm, we suggest things that interest you. No more unnecessary noise on your search page.

Unlock Rewards

Unlock rewards, badges and more as you take part in your favourite hobby. Winning badges is the best motivation for you to plug and play new hobbies.
